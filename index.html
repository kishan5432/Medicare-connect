Cardiology

Heart diseases such as heart attack, congestive heart failure, congenital heart disease, and coronary heart disease are the leading cause of death for men and women in every part of the globe.

At Medicare Connect, we provide integration with India's best heart hospitals which specialise in the treatment of heart disorders and ailments, making sure that all your cardiological needs are taken care of with the best-in-class treatments and state of the art services available.

Neurology

Neurology is the branch of medicine concerned with the study and treatment of disorders of the nervous system.

The neurologists with Medicare Connect are highly qualified and available to provide a wide range of inpatient services, including inpatient consults and treatment, and EEG and EMG tests.

Hepatology

Hepatology is a branch of medicine concerned with the study, prevention, diagnosis, and management of diseases that affect the liver, gallbladder, biliary tree, and pancreas.

Specialists in gastroenterology and hepatology with Medicare Connect have deep experience treating people with a wide range of diseases and disorders of the pancreas, liver, gallbladder, esophagus, stomach, small intestine and colon.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics is the branch of medicine dealing with the health and medical care of infants, children, and adolescents from birth up to the age of 18.

Pediatricians at Medicare Connect have a wide range of responsibilities. They do everything from perform well-baby checkups to manage chronic medical conditions. Pediatricians provide preventive care and also diagnose and treat infections, injuries and diseases.

Optometry

Optometry is a specialized health care profession that involves examining the eyes and related structures for defects or abnormalities.

The Medical Technology segment of Medicare Connect offers complete solutions to diagnose and treat ophthalmic diseases. In the field of microsurgery the our doctors provides innovative visualization solutions.
